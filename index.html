Information

Our protection system is designed to help reduce basic abuse, misuse, and small automated attacks for games, websites, and small projects.

What this protection DOES

  • Validates license / key access
  • Blocks fake or reused keys
  • Reduces spam and automated requests
  • Limits basic abuse attempts
  • Supports key expiration

What this protection DOES NOT do

  • Does not stop professional or advanced hackers
  • Does not protect against large DDoS attacks
  • Does not guarantee full security

Important:
No protection system is 100% secure. This service provides basic-level protection and should be used as an extra safety layer, not as a complete security solution.

By using this service, you accept its limits and understand that it reduces risks but does not remove them completely.
